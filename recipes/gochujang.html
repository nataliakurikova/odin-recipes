Sugar-free Gochujang

Description

Store-bought gochujang is typically high in sugar, this version is quite close in flavour profile but is sugar-free. Adapted from Meat Free Keto

Ingredients

  • 68 g (1/4 cup) miso
  • 20 g (1/4) cup Korean red pepper flakes (gochugaru)
  • 3 tbsp sugar-free maple syrup (eg Queen Maple Flavoured Sugar Free Syrup)
  • 2 tsp rice wine vinegar
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 80 g (1/3 cup) water

Steps

  1. Combine all ingrediants in a small saucepan, stirring well to remove any clumps
  2. Cook on low heat, stirring occasionally until the sauce thickens. This should take about 5 minutes
